About our Company

CEMEX Concrete Nigeria Limited

CEMEX CONCRETE Nigeria is one of the largest producers of ready-mix-concrete in Nigeria, with customers across Major Nigerian Cities. Our mission is simple: to provide the highest quality of concrete at an affordable price. We do this by offering our customers a wide range of materials, including cement, aggregate, sand and gravel as well as ready mixed concrete. We take pride in our ability to provide our clients with exceptional service and choice. We strive to be a reliable partner while providing exceptional value for money.
